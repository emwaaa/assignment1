Ingredients

1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup oil
1 1/2 cup sugar, plus 2 tablespoons
2 eggs
2 3/4 cup flour
2 teaspoons cream of tartar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons cinnamon

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. In a large bowl, combine the butter, oil, 1 1/2 cups sugar and the eggs and mix thoroughly until well combined.
3. Sift together the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda and salt, and stir into the shortening mixture.
4. In a small bowl, stir together the remaining 2 tablespoons sugar with the cinnamon.
5. Scoop 1 tablespoon of dough and form into a ball; roll each ball in the cinnamon-sugar mixture.
6. Bake at 400 degrees F for 8 to 10 minutes.
